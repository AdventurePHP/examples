APF sandbox

Thank you for choosing the Adventure PHP Framework (APF). We are sure to provide a development tool that let's you get to good results very fast.

The sandbox provides a quick step into the APF and contains the essential documentation pages as an offline version. Besides, the database config and template wizzard are intended to get in touch interactively. This enables you to enhance the sandbox page step by step.

In case of any questions please refer to the official documentation or post your question at the forum.
